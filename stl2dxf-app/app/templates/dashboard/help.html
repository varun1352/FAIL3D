Help Center

Quick Start Guide

  1. 1

    Upload your STL file

    Click the "UPLOAD STL" button on the dashboard and select your STL file.

  2. 2

    Preview the model

    Once uploaded, you can preview your 3D model and adjust the view as needed.

  3. 3

    Convert to DXF

    Click "Convert" to generate the DXF file. The process may take a few moments.

  4. 4

    Download the result

    Once conversion is complete, download your DXF file from the dashboard.

Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats are supported?

We currently support STL files (both binary and ASCII) for input and generate DXF files compatible with major CAD software.

How accurate is the conversion?

Our conversion algorithm maintains high accuracy while optimizing the output for practical use. You can adjust the quality settings for your specific needs.

What are the file size limits?

The maximum file size for STL uploads is 100MB. For larger files, please contact support.

Can I batch process multiple files?

Yes, you can upload multiple STL files and convert them to DXF format in batch.
